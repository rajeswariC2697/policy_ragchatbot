All reimbursement claims must be submitted through the expense management system
within 30 days of incurring the expense. Claims must be supported by valid receipts or
invoices.

Approval and Payment

Reimbursement claims are subject to manager approval. Approved claims are processed and
paid within 15 working days.

Non-Reimbursable Expenses

Personal expenses, fines, penalties, and expenses incurred without prior approval are not
eligible for reimbursement.

Extended Submission Window

For international business travel, reimbursement claims may be submitted within 60 days of
incurring the expense.

Work From Home (WFH) Policy

The company supports flexible work arrangements, including work from home, subject to
business requirements.

Eligibility

WFH is available to full-time employees who have completed at least 3 months of service
with the company.
