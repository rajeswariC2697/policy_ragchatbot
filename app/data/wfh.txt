Approval Process

Employees must obtain prior approval from their reporting manager before opting for WFH.
Approval may be granted on a full-day or partial-day basis.

Expectations

Employees working from home are expected to:

Be available during standard working hours

Attend all scheduled meetings

Maintain productivity and data security standards

Equipment and Expenses

The company may reimburse internet expenses for approved WFH arrangements, as per the
reimbursement policy.

Temporary Exception

New hires may be allowed to work from home during their first month of employment with
manager approval.
